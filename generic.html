About Me

Hey there! My name is Will Gephart and I am an aspiring Data Analyst currently living in Indianapolis, IN. I grew up in the Indiana area and went to school at Purdue University. After graduating, I began working as a health coach and over time decided that I would benefit from a change in career path which is what inspired me to go back to school to obtain my Master's Degree in Data Analytics from the University of Mississippi. Right now, I am continuing to build my skills and am working on projects in my free time so that I can develop a diverse skillset that will translate well to full time job setting.

In the time I have outside of work and classes I love spending time with my wife, Elle, and our dog, Arlo! Some of our favorite things to do include being outside, spending time with friends and family, and playing board games. Welcome to my website, and if there are any questions you have for me feel free to send me an email at wbgephart@gmail.com.
